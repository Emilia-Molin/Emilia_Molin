Digital twin for childcare

Digital twin

Research and design concept

The assignment was to create a digital twin, where I choose to design a partial digital twin for parents. The design consists of an application showing current information about the child, like sleep and growth, as well as future information calculated from growth patterns. The app is supposed to be connected to a piece of clothing made of electronic textiles that measures things like heart rate and sleep.
