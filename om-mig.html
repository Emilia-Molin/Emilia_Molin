My story as a design student

Art & Graphics

Art has always been a huge part of my life and something I have actively studied for 3 years before design. I have explored different ways to express myself and other themes through art, by mixing my technical understanding with a curiosity to try new and sometimes strange methods.

Research

Parallel to my art studies, I took a great interest in research, mainly psychologi and history, where I wanted to understand every part of why we are as we are, but also understand how history has shaped this. Through my research interest I have developed habits to be more effective when getting a broad understanding, as well as a good understanding of how to communicate what I know in text.

Design

Design became the obvious interest when deciding how to continue my passions, where I have been able to both research to understand my clients and their needs better, but also be able to creativly express myself through ideas and potential solutions.

Hej!

I am a digital design student at Kristianstad University that love all things art, design and research. My skills are mainly in grapich design, idea generation and research, but I'm activelt learning coding, webdesign, ux-design and more. As a designer I'm positive and never scared to try something new! :)
